01 / The Origin

The Obsidian Soil

High above the cloud line, on the fertile slopes of Mount Agung, our coffee cherries ripen in mineral-rich volcanic ash. This obsidian-dark soil infuses the beans with rare notes of dark cacao, smoke, and an intense earthy finish.

Elevation 1,700m ASL
Coordinates 8.3405° S, 115.5089° E
02 / The Roast

Born in Fire

Our beans are subjected to a rigorous roasting process that mirrors the volcanic origins of the soil. As the roasting fire cracks the shell, a burst of energy releases the locked-in natural oils, bringing forward a rich, smoky aroma and caramelized complexity.

Profile Double-Cracked
Roast Temp 228°C (442°F)
03 / The Pour

Cascading Alchemy

Precision extraction. We grind the obsidian beans to a velvet sand. When pure volcanic water meets the grounds, a cascade of deep brown notes is unleashed, locking in a pure flavor untainted by bitterness.

Grind Size Fine Sand
Water Ratio 1:15 Precision
